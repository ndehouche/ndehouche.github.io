Quick Bio

I hold a Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Operations Research from Paris-Dauphine University, and a State Engineer degree from Houari Boumedienne University of Science and Technology. My research interests revolve around Operations Research, Combinatorial Optimization, and Data Analysis. In a career spanning three continents, I have had the opportunity to undertake or participate in different projects in the fields of Energy, Non-Profit Project Management, Transportation, and Healthcare Management.
